This research line studies mega construction projects as socio-technical systems shaped by public concerns, stakeholder networks, risk uncertainty, and phase-specific governance demands.

Representative work includes an integrated topic and sentiment modeling approach for public concerns in mega construction projects {% cite jin2026public_concerns_mcp %}, stakeholder identification and evaluation through social network analysis and multi-criteria decision-making {% cite jin2025stakeholders_mcp %}, complexity measurement using fuzzy comprehensive evaluation and grey relational analysis {% cite jin2026complexity_mcp %}, and risk assessment through gray fuzzy group decision-making and Monte Carlo simulation {% cite jin2026risk_identification_mcp %}.
