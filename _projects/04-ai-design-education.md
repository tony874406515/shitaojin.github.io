My teaching and method-development work explores how AI can support architectural programming and design education, especially across problem seeking, information organization, decision-making, design iteration, and evaluation.

The central published case is an AI-assisted architectural programming and design course in _Buildings_ {% cite jin2024ai_architecture_education %}. This theme also connects to the broader architectural programming review and research agenda {% cite jin2025architectural_programming_comparative %} {% cite jin2025architectural_programming_interdisciplinary %}.
