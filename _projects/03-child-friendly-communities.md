This research extends architectural programming from individual buildings to community-scale social infrastructure. It focuses on child-friendly public environments, neighborhood centers, urban streets, and inclusive planning.

Representative studies include a value-based programming framework for children's well-being in urban communities {% cite tu2026value_based_programming %}, public perception analysis of Suzhou neighborhood center renewal {% cite deng2026suzhou_neighborhood_centers %}, and deep learning-based systems for evaluating child-friendly urban streets {% cite tu2025deep_learning_child_friendly %}.
