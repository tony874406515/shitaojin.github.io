Architectural programming is treated as a collaborative decision system rather than a static design brief. This research develops AI-enabled and group decision-making models to support preference learning, objective definition, and optimization in mega transportation and public projects.

The work builds from group decision-making for architectural programming in megaprojects {% cite tu2024group_decision_making %} toward multi-agent reinforcement learning for optimization in mega transportation projects {% cite jin2026collective_intelligence_caad %}. It also draws on a broader review of collective intelligence in the construction industry {% cite jin2025collective_intelligence_review %}.
